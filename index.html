About the Band

An indie band based in Cebu, Philippines.

Born from a golf ball that was found on the side of the street and a shattered glass window, Scheherazade upholds the values of vivid harmonic and melodic expression through their experimental style, utilizing the potential of simplicity in their creative musical endeavors. With a blend of strong personalities: Paul Divinagracia (Lead Vocals & Rhythm Guitar), Chadlester Codoy (Lead Guitar), Adam Holloway (Bass Guitar), Matt Cabrera (Keyboard), Anjel Fernandez (Drums), and Owen Aguas (Lyricist), the group solidifies their musical legacies through the nuance amongst their notes.
